A unique combination of authentic Asian food, a large selection of craft drinks, and plenty of fun times to go around is always the order of the day at Ginger Sports Bar. When you're looking for a sports bar in Denver, CO, which offers you the many great tastes of Asia in a fun and vibrant setting, you've come to the right place. 
 
We offer a wide array of Asian-inspired dishes, including Chinese, Vietnamese, Mongolian, and more to provide you with an eclectic dining experience like you've never had before. Our brand-new restaurant is the brainchild of a Chinese family that really knows all the ins and outs of Asian cooking. All of your favorites plus a number of lesser-known dishes fill our expansive menu.

We also have a large selection of domestic and craft brews on tap for your pleasure. Combine this with the vibrant atmosphere and fun times that accompany most sports bars, and you'll quickly discover there's really no reason to go anywhere else for an evening of great food and fine entertainment.
